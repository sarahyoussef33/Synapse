What's Synapse ?

Synapse is a digital health company offering innovative digital services for healthcare. We design simple, secure, and interoperable tools that enhance team collaboration and speed up clinical decisions for caregivers and patients.

What we deliver

  • Structured tele-expertise networks: clear workflows, auditable history, role-based access.
  • Security & compliance: privacy by design, consent management, healthcare-grade hosting, RGPD alignment.
  • Explainable AI triage: transparent criteria, human-in-the-loop, measurable impact on time-to-decision.

Who we serve ?

  • University hospitals
  • Private clinic groups
  • Mission-driven NGOs
  • National/Regional health programs
