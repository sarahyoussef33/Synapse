About Us

Synapse AI is a Lebanese digital healthcare company where doctors and healthcare developers join forces to transform the future of medicine. By combining clinical expertise with cutting-edge digital innovation, we deliver solutions that make healthcare smarter, faster, and more accessible.

Our team’s unique mix of medical insight and technical talent allows us to design platforms that truly serve both patients and providers—from optimizing hospital workflows and ensuring medication availability to empowering doctors with actionable data.

At Synapse AI, we don’t just build technology. We create digital healthcare experiences that save time, reduce costs, and improve lives.

Our Mission

To revolutionize healthcare in Lebanon and the region by delivering advanced digital solutions that make care smarter, faster, and personal. We bridge the gap between technology and medicine, empowering doctors, hospitals, and patients to thrive in a connected world.

Our Vision

To become the leading digital healthcare platform in Lebanon and the Middle East, setting the gold standard for innovation in healthcare. We envision a future where every hospital is smarter, every doctor is empowered, and every patient has access to world-class care—powered by intelligent digital solutions.

Privacy & Security

At Synapse AI, protecting patient data is non-negotiable. We apply international standards while aligning with Lebanon’s healthcare regulations.

  • Confidentiality: Patient records are strictly private, accessed only by authorized professionals.
  • Protection: All data is encrypted, monitored, and secured with role-based access.
  • Responsible AI: Our algorithms are designed with privacy safeguards and fairness checks.
  • Transparency: No hidden sharing—patients and partners know how data is used.

With Synapse, confidentiality isn’t just a policy; it’s our promise.
